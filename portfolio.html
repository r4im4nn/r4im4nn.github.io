WHO AM I

Hello, I'm r4im4nn. I've loved computer science for a very long time, at first I wanted to be a programmer, but then I discovered the huge world of cybersecurity when I was in high school. After high school I wanted to do long studies, but I realized after 2 years of studies (computer science/mathematics) and physics in 2nd year, that what I wanted was to work quickly in the field of cybersecurity without wasting my time on long studies, so I started working on my own, without looking at the hours I spent learning new things every day. I've become a rigorous and meticulous person. I share very interesting things I've learned on this website (courses, write-ups) and on my github.
